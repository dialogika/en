During A Month

During the program period, participants are required to attend 7 sessions of presentation class.

Online Based

Class will be held online twice a week on Zoom Cloud Meetings platform.

Level - II

This program is continuous from the Basic or Basic Plus

Problem Solved

Proper Opening

How to cope with boring introductions and opening during a presentation.

The Act upon Camera

How to represent yourself, gain confident, and look at the camera in a proper way.

Memorizing

The habit of memorizing presentation slides will usually lead to completely forgetting the materials when spoken.

Reading the Slide

The most common mistake is reading the slides rather than talking and interacting directly with the client.

Storytelling

The technique of presenting presentation materials smoothly as if we’re telling a story.

Insightful

How to state data, facts, and even including reliable sources aligning with the presentation materials.

Meeting Via Zoom

If you’re wondering, why don’t we have classes every day or every three days? For this program, we would like to give our participants more space to explore their own possibilities.

Syllabus

Materials for Each Meeting

00

Before
Meeting

Scheduling with mentor (time and date).

01

First Meeting:
Define Why

Exploring materials that would be used and learned throughout the whole program.

02

Second Meeting:
Design Essentials

We will be discussing about the essence of design for presentations and do practical learning.

03

Third Meeting:
Presentation Tactic

We will start into learning what kind of strategies that would be beneficial to use in general, at the office, and for thesis presentation.

04

Fourth Meeting:
Online Presentation

We will practice how to do online presentations by looking straight forward in the camera and remembering how to correctly use voice intonation.

05

Fifth Meeting:
Managing QnA

We will learn how to come up or manage answers for difficult and out of the box questions.

06

Sixth Meeting:
Data Storytelling

We will learn how to tell a story through the data that we have.

07

Last Meeting:
Final Test

This will be a practical test to see how participants can deliver and give a good presentation.

Post Program

What happens once you finished all your classes and completed this program?

CONTINOUS MENTORING

After you have completed your class, you will still have the right to consult and discuss with your mentor and other members regarding your public speaking concerns.

ECERTIFICATE

After completion, as a member, you will be granted with an e-certificate that will prove your participation and a skill improvement in public speaking.

EVENT PARTICIPANTS

In addition, members will also have the chance to be recruited as a moderator, master of ceremony, or facilitator for our future events on a professional or even national level.

Frequently Asked Questions

For those of you who are curious about our program details

  • What is Online Class – Presentation?

    Online Presentation Class is a continuous, second level program from Basic or Basic Plus that focuses on learning how to carry a presentation in front of public.

  • Wow, so this class is level 2. What can I gain from it?

    Besides having a month-long period for the whole program, in this class, we will do more practical learning.

  • If this is level 2, can I apply without having to take the first level class first?

    Yes, you can but we don’t recommend it. Instead, we highly recommend you to take Basic or Basic Plus Class first, since this class will be a lot more technical. We worry that the storytelling by data, elaboration, expression, and hand gestures technique might not be effective to learn if participants are missing out on basic class materials.

  • Okay2, I get it. So, how many people would there be in a single class?

    A class will consist of maximum 7 people only.

Vibes, Process & Culture

The demand of a good public speaking skill during this pandemic is becoming even more crucial and essential.

This is caused by the rising numbers of online meetings and how offline meetings are becoming very short-lived. Frequent miscommunications are one of the most general bad effects that happens often, so now, failure is not the option anymore.

Furthermore, the culture of blaming others that can be found in both office or college environments resulted in increasing people’s fear to explore their communication capabilities.

Dialogika is created to give a safe space for anyone, who seek to learn and develop themselves in terms of communication, especially for public speaking. To those feeling trapped in the negative vibes of their work place and having a mental block, Dialogika might also be the right answer for you.

Here, we will all learn together to …

Appreciate each other’s progress

Often times, we will forget to appreciate every little progress that we make ourselves, which might lead to a decrease on self-esteem.

Express yourself

In Dialogika, you have to try to express your true colors and never be afraid to try something new.

Familiarize yourself with fear

Being familiar with a particular problem would make the defense mechanism in our brain to later tackle on that problem. In Dialogika, we will try to create that mechanism by coping it with confidence.


All of the things above would be hard to achieve on your own, but with the help of a small circle and support system, this is possible! That is also the reason why we are able to build Dialogika as a space for all of you to learn public speaking until you can!!
