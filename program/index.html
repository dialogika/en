F.A.Q

Learn more here

  • How is the Public Speaking teaching system at Dialogika?

    A good learning system is when you are given space to practice and explore yourself. Dialogika provides access and freedom to practice throughout the course. Even after the class ends (for some programs), Dialogika also offers the opportunity for you to ask questions with the mentors at Dialogika.

  • Why is the class in a cafe? Not in a room?

    Because we want participants to practice with the element of surprise or unexpected shock, and throughout our 3-year research, we realized that mastering the element of surprise was essential to helping our members conquer the feeling of blanking out.

    The unexpected surprise we're referring to is like the hustle and bustle of people, and the sudden sounds that can be a bit distracting to your concentration

    We believe that by conquering the element of surprise, your confidence will gradually improve.

  • Is there a guarantee for the results?

    We provide a guarantee for the various learning programs we offer, ranging from Basic Public Speaking, Presentation, MC, to Pitching. If you do not feel any improvement with us, you can retake the class for FREE WITHOUT ANY CHARGE.

  • Have you (Dialogika) ever handled clients from all kinds of professions?

    If you are serious about improving your Public Speaking skills and need our help, we can assist you.

    Are you an introvert and want to have the courage to speak, and need our help? We can assist you.

    Bad trauma haunts you; presenting in front of many people is not your true self. But deep down you know that you need to fix this problem, and you may need our help with that. We're here to assist you. (As sweet as gummy bears with the mental fortitude of real bears, we will guide you with care and help you get there as well!)

    You are a mature individual, and your position in the company or government agency is quite high. Nervousness is not your problem, but you cannot motivate your subordinates, and your speech tends to jump around, making your employees unable to work according to your expectations. We can help you.

    You are a successful entrepreneur, often asked to speak in front of many people. But you feel that what is in your mind does not match what your mouth says. Do you need us? We can help you.

    Currently, you are almost graduating from the Medical Department, but because you have a Javanese accent, your speech tends to sound less professional. Do you need help? We can assist you.

    No matter what your current profession is, whether you are a student, a college student, a fresh graduate, an employee, a professional, a freelancer, a leader, an entrepreneur, or a party cadre, if you want to improve your public speaking skills, have problems with communication, or have personal issues, we are ready to help you solve your problems.

  • What happens if I am unable to attend the class at the scheduled time?

    There are two conditions if you cannot attend the class. The first condition is for the general program (first class), and the second condition is for the private program (first private).

    First Class Program: If you are unable to attend the first-class program, please report to our Class Admin no later than two days before the class is held. We will provide a makeup class in the next batch for the class you missed.

    First Private Program: If you are unable to attend the first private class, the class will be rescheduled on the condition that you inform the Class Admin at the latest 2 days before the class. If there is no notification, the meeting will be skipped, and participants will receive a replacement class by joining the first class (general) session that was missed.
